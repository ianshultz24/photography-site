About

Ian Shultz

I'm a bird photographer and student focused on the conservation of threatened species and habitats. My work focuses on capturing simple, peaceful moments in time, like the quiet stillness of dawn and the raw power of flight.

Every image is the product of patience, fieldcraft, and a deep respect for the natural world. I shoot primarily with long telephoto lenses in natural light, prioritizing authenticity over everything.
